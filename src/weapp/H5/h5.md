### 1. 监听当前用户是安卓还是ios

```js
getUserAgent = navigator.userAgent;
isAndroid = getUserAgent.indexOf('Android') > -1 || getUserAgent.indexOf('Adr') > -1; // android终端
isiOS = !!getUserAgent.match(/\(i[^;]+;( U;)? CPU.+Mac OS X/); // ios终端
```
